# Elysian Party Program

Elysian party program provides many services such as:
- Wedding planning
- Birthday planning
- Graduation planning

The system allows the customer to choose one of the services provided and enter PartDetails like:
- Cake
- Decoration
- Drinks
- Photographs
- Events
- Invitation Design

After selecting the necessary services, the program will print a customer receipt on the screen, showing the customer's name, mobile number, and the service needed with its price. It also asks the customer to rate the service by entering a value from 1 to 5.

Furthermore, the system offers discounts to Umm Al-Qura University students by 15% of the total price and 10% to curlIT Salon.
